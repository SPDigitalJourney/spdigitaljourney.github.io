5 Legal Basis for Processing

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, SPDigitalJourney processes your personal data on the following bases:

  • Contract performance: Processing is necessary to deliver services you have engaged SPDigitalJourney for.

  • Legitimate interest: Anonymised performance data used to improve services, with no impact on individual rights.

  • Financial records: Retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax law.

8 Data Security

SPDigitalJourney takes reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, including:

  • Password-protected access to all client files and records
  • Platform access granted only on a need-to-perform basis and revoked upon project completion
  • No storage of client login credentials in plain text

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. SPDigitalJourney cannot guarantee absolute security but commits to notifying affected clients of any data breach within 72 hours of becoming aware of it.

9 Your Rights

Under India's DPDPA 2023, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the personal data SPDigitalJourney holds about you.
  • Right to correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for processing at any time. This does not affect processing carried out before withdrawal.
  • Right to grievance redressal: Raise a complaint with SPDigitalJourney directly; if unresolved, with the Data Protection Board of India.
To exercise any of these rights, contact SPDigitalJourney via WhatsApp (+91 77994 23794) or email (rpsanjaypaul@gmail.com). Requests will be acknowledged within 72 hours and resolved within 30 days.
