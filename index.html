The Visibility Problem

Most Bengaluru businesses are invisible on Google Maps.

A Google listing alone does not bring customers. Without the right ranking signals, reviews, and structure — competitors rank above you every time someone searches.

📍
Not appearing in "near me" searches
Google ranks by relevance, distance, and prominence. Miss any signal — and the Local Pack top 3 (where 70% of clicks go) belongs to competitors.
⭐
Weak reviews hurting trust and ranking
Review volume, velocity, and quality are top ranking signals. Low or stagnant reviews tell Google — and customers — that the business is not active.
📋
Wrong category and missing keywords
GBP category is the single biggest ranking signal most businesses misconfigure. Wrong category means invisible for most searches in Bengaluru.
📱
Profile views that never become calls
Visibility without conversion is wasted effort. Photo quality, call path structure, and profile completeness decide whether visitors call or scroll past.
