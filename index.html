// The Origin Story

Built by a
Google Maps Insider

SPDigitalJourney was created by someone who didn't just study Google Maps — he helped build it. As a member of the Google Maps India Hyderabad Division, Sanjay Paul was part of the historic transition from paper maps to digital navigation in 2007.

His contributions to Google Maps India include road mapping, street name & numbering, house and postal code numbering, National Highway, State Highway and inter-state highway mapping, global mapping development, Street View navigation, user-reported issue fixing, and community support.

Later, as a Google Street View photographer, Sanjay visited 100+ cities across 7+ states, scanning 2,000+ business properties across every category — retail, healthcare, education, hospitality, F&B, manufacturing and more — publishing high-quality Google Preferred photos and 360° videos.
