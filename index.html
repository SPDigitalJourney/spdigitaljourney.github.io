The Visibility Problem

Most businesses are
invisible on Google Maps.

A listing alone doesn't guarantee customers. Without the right signals, reviews, and structure — your competitors rank above you every time.

  • 📍
    Not showing in "near me" searches

    Google ranks by relevance, distance, and prominence. Missing any one of these costs you the top 3 pack — where 70% of clicks go.

  • ⭐
    Weak reviews killing trust

    Customers read reviews before calling. Low volume or poor responses signal a business that doesn't care — and Google agrees.

  • 📋
    Wrong category and keywords

    Google Business Profile categories are the single biggest ranking signal most businesses get wrong — often from day one.

  • 📱
    Visitors who don't convert to calls

    Traffic without a clear call path is wasted visibility. Profile structure, photos, and CTAs determine whether they call or scroll past.
