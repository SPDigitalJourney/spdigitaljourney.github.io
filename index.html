The Visibility Problem

Most Bengaluru businesses are invisible on Google Maps.

A Google listing alone doesn't bring customers. Without the right ranking signals, reviews, and structure — competitors rank above you every time someone searches.

  • 📍
    Not appearing in "near me" searches

    Google ranks by relevance, distance, and prominence. Miss any one — and the Local Pack top 3 (where 70% of clicks go) belongs to competitors.

  • ⭐
    Weak reviews hurting trust and ranking

    Review volume, velocity, and quality are top ranking signals. Low or stagnant reviews tell Google — and customers — your business isn't active.

  • 📋
    Wrong category and missing keywords

    GBP category is the single biggest ranking signal most businesses misconfigure. Wrong category = invisible for most searches.

  • 📱
    Profile views that never become calls

    Visibility without conversion is wasted effort. Photo quality, call path structure, and profile completeness decide whether visitors call or scroll past.
