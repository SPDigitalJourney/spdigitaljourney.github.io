Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to the most common questions business owners ask.

Do you take logins or manage accounts?

No. You stay in full control of your accounts. I only guide you on what to do and why.

How much does a session cost?

There is no fixed fee. You may contribute any amount based on your comfort and the value you receive. Most contributions range between ₹500 and ₹1,000.

How long is a session?

Mini session: 20–30 minutes. Full guidance session: 45–60 minutes.

Do you upload photos or content for me?

No. I guide you on what works best, but all uploads and actions are done by you.

Can you help any type of business?

Yes — clinics, schools, salons, service providers, shops, tuition centers, coaching institutes, and more. If Google shows your business, I can help you understand it better.

Is this marketing, SEO, or ads?

No. This is guidance based on Google’s visibility, relevance and trust signals — not ad campaigns or paid promotions.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes — you can reach out anytime for follow‑up questions or clarity. I’m here to guide, not to take control.
