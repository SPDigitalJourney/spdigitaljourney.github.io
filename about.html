About SPDigitalJourney

My name is Sanjay Paul, a Digital Marketer and Google Ecosystem Specialist with over 15 years of experience working across Google Maps, Business Profiles, Street View, and local discovery systems.

I was part of the 2007 Google Maps Pilot Batch, contributing to the transformation from paper maps to digital mapping. Over the years, I’ve helped thousands of business owners understand how Google sees their business — and how customers discover them.

My Background

  • 15+ years working with Google Maps & Business Profiles
  • 2000+ Street View projects contributed
  • 5000+ business owners guided across India
  • Experience in road mapping, address structuring, and local discovery systems
  • Active contributor to Google Help Communities

Why I Started SPDigitalJourney

Most business owners don’t struggle because of lack of effort — they struggle because Google’s systems are misunderstood. SPDigitalJourney was created to bridge that gap through clear, ethical, no‑login guidance.

  • Businesses should own and understand their digital presence
  • Guidance builds confidence and independence
  • Ethical practices create long‑term, sustainable growth

My goal is simple: Help you understand how Google sees your business — so you can improve it with clarity and confidence.
