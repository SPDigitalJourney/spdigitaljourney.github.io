Operational Parameters

Terms of Service & Framework

Legal architecture governing professional local data mapping, optimization protocols, and consulting operations.

1. Nature of Services

SP Digital Journey provides technical consulting, data accuracy optimization, spatial media processing, and local discovery configuration. All architectural optimizations are performed adhering to the public frameworks and interface conditions of global mapping platforms (such as Google Maps, Google Business Profile, and underlying data registries).

2. Independent Operator Status

While our engineering background traces directly back to foundational map structures (including historical operations within the Google Maps India, Hyderabad Division), SP Digital Journey operates entirely as an independent technology and service provider. We are not legally affiliated with, endorsed by, or an official subsidiary of Google LLC or Alphabet Inc.

3. Platform Limitations & Core Liabilities

Local visibility metrics and platform algorithmic indexing are subject to updates completely managed by third-party search engines. SP Digital Journey ensures technical deployment accuracy according to industry-standard protocols. However, structural map platform behavior, listing suspensions due to systemic algorithm sweeps, automated verifications, or individual data display processing speed remain entirely under the structural domain of the respective directory platform.

4. MSME Framework Compliance

SP Digital Journey operates transparently within the Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSME) regulatory framework of India. All formal operations, corporate consulting agreements, service delivery invoices, and support schedules are bound by the legal standards of this ecosystem. Any transactional disputes are governed exclusively under regional jurisdiction frameworks.

5. Client Responsibilities

Clients seeking verification assistance or profile correction must provide verified, accurate physical ownership data, legal registry certifications, or operational site records. Providing fraudulent business locations, fabricated documentation, or engaging in forbidden local spam configurations breaks our operational safety protocol and will result in the immediate cancellation of active optimization tickets.
